Luis Gustavo Rivera de Leon

Malice Ruins

As much as I try to be a positive person and uplift others, sometimes I just feel like letting everything burn. I drew this while in a very dark and violent place. Most people don't enjoy being angry but I love to fight, I get a certain bloodlust for anger once I get pushed far enough. I think it's because people become more open and raw and passionate when they're angry, and this bloodmoon is the culmination of all of that emotion.
